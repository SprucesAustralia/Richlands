Scope of Work

Site/General
Task:
Initial Preparation & Safety Compliance - Conduct a thorough site inspection to identify access restrictions, hazardous areas, or special cleaning requirements. - Confirm all major construction debris and waste have been removed by the builder or contractor. - Coordinate with other trades.
Notes:
- Ensure all cleaning personnel are inducted and equipped with proper PPE. - Verify compliance with relevant Australian Standards, NCC, and local codes.
Tools:
- Inspection checklist - PPE (hard hats, gloves, goggles, etc.) - Communication devices
Internal Areas
Task:
General Internal Cleaning - Remove all loose dust, dirt, and construction residue from floors, walls, and ceilings. - Vacuum carpeted areas and dust off tiled, vinyl, and timber floors. - Mop hard floor surfaces; clean doors, door frames, handles, and stops. - Wipe skirting boards, window sills, and horizontal ledges. - Dust internal signages, outlets, switches, fans, and AC vents. - Spot clean walls and vacuum stairwells (including handrails and balustrades).
Notes:
- Pay special attention to areas with heavy construction residue or adhesive marks. - Ensure all surfaces are free of smudges and fingerprints.
Tools:
- Vacuum cleaner - Mops and buckets - Dusters and microfiber cloths - All-purpose cleaning agents
Windows & Glazing
Task:
Cleaning of Windows & Glazing - Clean all internal windows including frames, tracks, and sills. - Remove protective tape and any adhesive residue. - Wipe down glazing panels, partitions, and mirrors.
Notes:
- Ensure windows operate smoothly and have a streak-free finish.
Tools:
- Glass cleaner - Microfiber cloths - Squeegee
Joinery & Fixtures
Task:
Cleaning Joinery & Fixtures - Dust and wipe down all joinery such as cabinetry, shelves, and cupboard interiors/exteriors. - Clean fixtures including door handles, light switches, and power outlets. - Clean basic kitchen surfaces, benchtops, and sinks.
Notes:
- In-depth appliance cleaning is not part of the standard scope.
Tools:
- Soft cloths - Mild detergent - Dusters
Sanitary Areas
Task:
Sanitary Areas Cleaning (Bathrooms, Powder Rooms, Common Amenities) - Clean and disinfect toilets, urinals, sinks, and taps. - Wipe down vanities, mirrors, towel rails, and soap holders. - Clean shower screens and remove any protective stickers. - Mop and sanitize tiled/vinyl floors.
Notes:
- Emphasize hygiene and sanitation, especially on high-touch surfaces.
Tools:
- Disinfectants - Scrub brushes - Mops - Cleaning cloths - Gloves
External Areas
Task:
Balconies, Patios & Outdoor Cleaning - Sweep and remove loose dust and debris from balconies and outdoor areas. - Lightly wash flooring to remove dust and residue. - Remove any construction rubbish.
Notes:
- Ensure all outdoor areas are clear of construction waste and ready for use.
Tools:
- Brooms - Outdoor cleaning solutions - Water hose or pressure washer (if required)
Site Completion
Task:
Final Checks & Waste Disposal - Place all cleaning-related waste into designated bins. - Ensure the entire site is left tidy and ready for inspection.
Notes:
- No specialized treatments or deep polishing are included at this stage.
Tools:
- Waste bins - Disposal bags - Final inspection checklist
