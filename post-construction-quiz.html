Tips for Post-Construction Cleaning

  1. Start by removing all large debris and construction materials from the site.
  2. Work from top to bottom to prevent re-contaminating cleaned areas.
  3. Use a HEPA filter vacuum to capture fine construction dust effectively.
  4. Clean windows and glass surfaces before tackling floors to avoid dripping debris.
  5. Use microfiber cloths for dusting to trap particles instead of spreading them.
  6. Ensure proper ventilation when using cleaning chemicals to maintain air quality.
  7. Pay special attention to corners and edges where dust tends to accumulate.
  8. Use dry methods before wet cleaning to avoid creating mud from construction dust.
  9. Clean and store all tools properly after use to maintain their condition.
  10. Conduct a final walk-through to ensure all areas meet cleanliness standards.
